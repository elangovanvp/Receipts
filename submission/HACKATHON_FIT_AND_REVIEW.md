## Judging rubric — axis by axis (each 25%)


### Product Thinking  —  self-score 23 / 25

**How Receipts meets it:** Best-in-class on this axis. The problem is real and SOURCED, not invented: verbatim r/ProductManagement pain ('confabulated fairy tales,' 'fed ChatGPT release notes... still got hallucinations and went back to reading them myself'). The persona is named and narrow (solo/early-stage PMs, founders doing a pre-build check) rather than 'everyone.' The wedge — epistemic honesty / refuse-to-assert-without-a-source — is a genuine product INSIGHT, not a feature list, and no incumbent owns it. WTP narrative is explicit: an existing paid CI market (Crayon/Klue/Kompyte, ~$15-30k/yr, labelled illustrative) that ignores the low end. Scope is ruthlessly cut (no accounts, no saved workspaces, no CRM, no payments) — exactly Killeen's 'ruthless about what you ask for.'


**Gaps / risks a skeptical judge will probe:** Two soft spots a skeptical judge will probe: (a) is a one-shot, 3-5 page fetch genuinely a substitute for continuous CI, or is it a 'quick gut-check' that PMs use once and abandon (the same 'shelved matrix' fate it critiques)? (b) The honest failure modes (site blocks fetch, thin public info, ambiguous name) mean output quality is target-dependent; a judge who tests an obscure or JS-heavy product may get a sparse teardown and read it as 'thin' rather than 'honest.' The brand turns that into a virtue, but only if the empty-state copy lands.


### Craft & Execution  —  self-score 21 / 25

**How Receipts meets it:** Very high ceiling and visibly intentional. 'The Dossier' detective case-file system (clay/paper palette, Fraunces+Inter+Geist Mono, wax-seal hero, Exhibit receipt-stamp chips, amber highlighter on corroborated claims, redaction bars on the Couldn't-verify block, word-by-word claim reveal) is coherent and opinionated, not template AI-slop. The CaseFileGathering loading state that streams REAL tool calls (SEARCH/FETCH log, SEARCHING->FETCHING->SYNTHESIZING) makes the core claim ('I am reading real pages now') tangible — strong for the two design/usability judges. Reduced-motion safety is handled (opacity-only fallbacks). Synthesis prompt is genuinely well-engineered (verbatim-quote enforcement, weaknesses-must-bite, no fetch-errors-as-weaknesses). Build passes, no console errors, no mobile horizontal overflow per the build log.


**Gaps / risks a skeptical judge will probe:** 'Works end-to-end on the public URL' is the literal craft test and the URL is not live yet — so craft is currently unverifiable by a stranger, which is exactly what this axis demands. Live-fetch flakiness (Jina keyless throttling, JS-heavy sites, 60s maxDuration on Vercel nodejs) could produce a sparse or stalled run during judging. The 3 example targets are described as pre-cached for demo determinism, but ad-hoc judge-entered targets are not. Heavy Framer Motion + Lenis raises a small jank/perf risk on low-end judging hardware.


### Originality & Ambition  —  self-score 23 / 25

**How Receipts meets it:** This is the standout axis. 'Epistemic honesty as the product' is a values-statement disguised as a feature — the visible 'Couldn't verify' block is the rare AI demo that brags about what it WON'T say. That is surprising, distinctly the maker's own, and directly echoes Dreimann's 'trust in AI' essay and 'nothing reaches production without approval' (the Couldn't-verify block is literally the human-in-the-loop deferral). The deliberate model-agnostic TEXT protocol (one line at a time, because open models mis-format native tool calls) and the two-model split (Llama 3.3 70B research, GPT-OSS 120B synthesis) are an honest, defensible engineering point of view rather than novelty for its own sake.


**Gaps / risks a skeptical judge will probe:** Ambition is deliberately capped (one screen, one output) — correct strategy under this rubric, but a judge optimizing for 'make us sit up' could find the surface area modest versus flashier multi-feature entries. The cited-output pattern (RAG + inline citations) is not itself novel in the broad AI market; the originality lives in the PRODUCT FRAMING and the refuse-to-assert doctrine, so the demo/video must sell the doctrine, not the tech, or the originality reads as 'another citations wrapper.'


### Shippedness  —  self-score 12 / 25

**How Receipts meets it:** The architecture is built for shippedness on paper: no-login flow, permalink-only persistence, a bundled real Linear sample so the UI works with zero API keys, and Novus instrumentation deliberately designed to look agentic (3-7 visible tool calls/session, two models, a clear goal event teardown_shared_or_exported, and a landed->submitted->rendered->shared funnel). The trackAgent/trackGoalShare call-sites in src/lib/run.ts are correctly placed on prompt, every completion path (done/error/unsupported), and share — so data WILL flow once the SDK loads.


**Gaps / risks a skeptical judge will probe:** This is where Receipts is currently weakest and most at risk. As of ~7.5h before deadline: (1) NOT deployed — no public vercel.app URL; SUBMISSION.md has placeholder text. The rubric's defining test ('a stranger can use it now') currently fails. (2) Novus is NOT installed — track.ts is pure no-op and layout.tsx has no Pendo <Script>; window.pendo never exists, so the dashboard is EMPTY and the project is INELIGIBLE until fixed. (3) No demo video, no Novus screenshot. (4) Even after install, Novus batches hourly — traffic must be seeded >1h pre-judging or the screenshot shows nothing. Shippedness is 25% of the score and is the axis most exposed; honest score reflects 'excellent scaffolding, mandatory artifacts unfinished, thin runway.'

## Red flags to manage

- TIME/EXECUTION RISK is the dominant red flag: ~7.5h to the 16:00 UTC deadline with deploy, Novus install, traffic seeding, screenshot, and video ALL still to do. Sequencing matters because Novus batches hourly — a late install means an empty dashboard screenshot at judging.
- ELIGIBILITY RISK: Novus is genuinely not installed (track.ts is no-op only; no Pendo <Script> in layout.tsx). The rules state projects without Novus are INELIGIBLE. This is currently a hard fail, not a deduction.
- STACK DRIFT between docs and code (now mostly resolved): 3-day-old memory described a Claude Opus + native Anthropic web_search/web_fetch build needing ANTHROPIC_API_KEY; the current repo and brief are the Groq/Llama+GPT-OSS/Jina text-protocol build. @anthropic-ai/sdk is still a listed dependency but appears unused by the live route — a judge inspecting the repo could find it confusing; consider removing it.
- OUTPUT-QUALITY-IS-TARGET-DEPENDENT risk: keyless Jina + JS-heavy or fetch-blocking sites + 60s maxDuration means an ad-hoc judge target can yield a sparse teardown. The 'Couldn't verify' framing converts this to a virtue ONLY if the empty-state copy is convincing; otherwise it reads as a thin/broken tool.
- SELF-REFERENTIAL CREDIBILITY trap: a product whose whole thesis is 'no unsourced claims' must hold its OWN pitch to that bar. Any fabricated traction, user count, or unlabelled market number in the video/description would be disproportionately damaging — exactly what Michelson/Dreimann would catch.
- SUBSTITUTION question a judge may raise: a one-shot teardown may share the fate it mocks ('matrices get shelved') — used once, not habitually — weakening the WTP/retention story even as it strengthens the instant-value story.
